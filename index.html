Hi there, Thanks for checking out my portfolio!

I am a recent graduate from MSU with a bachelor's degree in Games and Interactive Media. I have been programming since I was 12 and have always had a passion for games and how they are made. In my senior year of high school, I took a game design class, and ever since then, I've known it's what I want to do. While studying at Michigan State, I had the opportunity to work on multiple games, including Sneakeasy by Spartasoft Studios and Bears Hug, which was made for a game jam. I have experience in scripting, level design, and system design. I also have quite a bit of experience designing and building websites.

Previous Work

Sneakeasy

This game was my first experience working with a large team on a game. My role on the team was scripting enemy AI and pathfinding as well as helping program detection systems.

Bears Hug

This game was developed during a game jam hosted by MSU. My role in making this game was all over the place from brainstorming and design to programming enemy AI. The game was well received and won best overall during judging.

Tower Offensive

This was another Game jam game that I worked on for the 2023 GMTK Game Jam. For this project, I didn't have as much time to devote to the game jam as I would have liked, so it didn't turn out quite as well as some of my other work. For this game, I helped program AI and UI as well as tower placement.
